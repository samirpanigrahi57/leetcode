## Position Summary

Data steward with deep subject matter expertise for Medical Economics data assets. Analyzes complex healthcare information, maintains business definitions and data logic, translates stakeholder needs into structured requirements, designs data models, and conducts validation checks across systems.

## Key Responsibilities

- Serve as data steward for Medical Economics data assets with expert-level SQL skills
- Analyze complex healthcare data and maintain business definitions and data logic
- Translate business stakeholder needs into structured data requirements
- Design data models and conduct validation checks across multiple systems
- Identify data quality issues and drive resolution with engineering partners
- Communicate findings to senior leadership and cross-functional teams
- Support service categorization and authorization data initiatives

## Required Qualifications

- Bachelor's degree in Finance, Economics, Computer Science, or related field
- 6+ years in Data, Finance, or Systems Analysis roles
- Expert knowledge of SQL

## Preferred Qualifications

- Medical Economics experience
- Business stakeholder management skills
- Python, R, or BI tools (Tableau, Power BI)
- Service categorization and authorization data expertise
