## Position Summary

Conducts analysis of healthcare claims and pharmacy data to evaluate network utilization and cost containment. Develops reports using SQL, Databricks, and Power BI; identifies data anomalies and trends; communicates findings to senior leadership; manages special projects for internal departments and regulatory agencies.

## Key Responsibilities

- Analyze healthcare claims and pharmacy data for network utilization and cost containment evaluation
- Develop reports using SQL, Databricks, and Power BI
- Identify data anomalies and trends in complex healthcare datasets
- Communicate findings and insights to senior leadership
- Manage special projects for internal departments and regulatory reporting
- Support data quality improvement initiatives

## Required Qualifications

- Bachelor's degree in Finance, Economics, Computer Science, or related field
- 5–7 years managing complex databases
- 5–7 years analyzing financial/performance metrics in healthcare
- Advanced SQL knowledge

## Preferred Qualifications

- Databricks experience
- Python coding skills
- Background in medical economics and predictive modeling

Role requires Eastern Time working hours (not flexible to other time zones).
