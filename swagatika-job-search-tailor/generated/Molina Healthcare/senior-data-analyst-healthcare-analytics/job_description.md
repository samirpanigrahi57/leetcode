# Senior Data Analyst, Healthcare Analytics — Molina Healthcare

**Source:** Official Molina Healthcare Careers (careers.molinahealthcare.com)
**Posted:** April 28, 2026
**Location:** United States (Remote)

**Salary:** $59,810.60 – $129,589.63 annually (varies by location)

## Position Summary

Performs research and analysis of complex healthcare claims data, vendor data, financial data, pharmacy data, and lab data. Develops ad-hoc and standard reports to support internal departments, regulatory agencies, and the enterprise. Manages SharePoint documentation and reporting processes.

## Key Responsibilities

- Perform research and analysis of complex healthcare claims, vendor, financial, pharmacy, and lab data
- Develop ad-hoc and standard reports using SQL, SSRS, Excel, and analytics tools
- Analyze data trends for anomalies and forecast future patterns using statistical methodologies
- Manage SharePoint sites and document reporting processes
- Support internal departments and respond to regulatory reporting requirements
- Present findings and insights to business stakeholders and leadership

## Required Qualifications

- Bachelor's degree in Finance, Economics, Computer Science, or related field
- 5–7 years of database management and financial metrics analysis experience
- Healthcare experience quantifying and analyzing financial/utilization metrics
- Advanced SQL proficiency for data analysis and predictive work

## Preferred Qualifications

- Medical economics knowledge
- Experience with BI tools (Power BI, Tableau)
- Background in healthcare analytics
- Python or statistical modeling experience
