## Position Summary

Analyzes integrated and extensive datasets to extract value that impacts business decisions. Works collaboratively with key business stakeholders to identify areas of value, develop solutions, and deliver insights to reduce overall cost of care for members and improve clinical outcomes. Focus on provider analytics — network utilization, provider performance, and cost-of-care analysis.

## Key Responsibilities

- Analyze large integrated healthcare datasets with a focus on provider analytics
- Collaborate with business stakeholders to identify data-driven opportunities
- Develop insights to reduce cost of care and improve clinical outcomes
- Apply SQL and analytical tools to extract, transform, and model data
- Build and maintain BI reports and dashboards (Power BI preferred)
- Conduct trend analysis and root-cause analysis on healthcare data anomalies
- Document data definitions, methodologies, and reporting logic

## Required Qualifications

- Working knowledge of SQL/querying languages
- Healthcare analytics experience preferred
- Authorized to work in the U.S. without employment-based visa sponsorship

## Preferred Qualifications

- Python or R programming knowledge
- Statistical and analytical techniques (data modeling, trend analysis, root-cause analysis)
- Microsoft Power BI or equivalent BI/visualization tools
- Healthcare domain knowledge — provider data, network analytics, cost-of-care
